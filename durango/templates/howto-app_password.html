• What is an App password?

In order to allow Durango to access your mail securely, you need to generate an app password from your mail account. Following tutorial show how to generate one on gmail.

• How to create & use App Passwords from your Google account?

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. On the left navigation panel, choose Security.
  3. On the "Signing in to Google" panel, choose App Passwords. If you don’t see this option:
    • 2-Step Verification is not set up for your account.
    • 2-Step Verification is set up for security keys only.
    • Your account is through work, school, or other organization.
    • You’ve turned on Advanced Protection for your account.
  4. At the bottom, choose Select app and choose the app you’re using.
  5. Choose Generate.
  6. Follow the instructions to enter the App Password. The App Password is the 16-character code in the yellow bar on your device.
  7. Choose Done.
You’ll only have to enter an App Password once per app or device, so don’t worry about memorizing it.

• Managing App-Specific Passwords

The value of an application-specific password is that you can revoke and regenerate a password on a service-by-service basis instead of having to change the master password to your account. You are free to generate as many app-specific passwords as you like.
